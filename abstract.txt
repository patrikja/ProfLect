Inaugural lecture by Patrik Jansson

Patrik Jansson at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering will hold his lecture for professorship, entitled "Strongly Typed Programs and Proofs"

I will present highlights from 20 years of scientific adventures in the world of strongly typed functional programming, domain specific languages and even global systems science.

​Patrik Jansson is head of the Software Technology division at Computer Science and Engineering.

* Category: Public lecture
* Location: EC, EDIT-building, Rännvägen 6, Johanneberg.
* Starts: 21 August, 2015, 13:00
* Ends: 21 August, 2015, 14:00
